Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our marketing services.

How long does a typical marketing campaign take?

The duration of a marketing campaign varies based on your goals and the strategies employed. Most campaigns show initial results within 4-6 weeks, with optimal performance achieved after 3-6 months of continuous optimization.

What's the difference between organic and paid campaigns?

Organic campaigns focus on building long-term visibility through content, SEO, and social media without direct advertising costs. Paid campaigns use advertising budgets to achieve immediate visibility and results through platforms like Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and other paid channels.

How do you measure campaign success?

We use a combination of metrics including website traffic, conversion rates, engagement metrics, ROI, and specific KPIs aligned with your business objectives. We provide detailed monthly reports with insights and recommendations for optimization.

Do you work with businesses in specific industries?

We have experience across multiple industries including technology, e-commerce, healthcare, professional services, and more. Our strategies are tailored to your specific industry and target audience.
